Project Overview
This was a personal project, and something of a rite of passage for many Blender users. The infamous "donut tutorial" is a 14-part YouTube tutorial made by YouTuber BlenderGuru. Though I had used Blender in my 3D animation class in high school, I wanted to broaden my horizons on what tools were available to me.
